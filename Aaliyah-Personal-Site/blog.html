Mini Blog

My journey to becoming a web developer has been quite a ride. I feel that I should start at the point when my interest of computers peaked. I had always been interested in computers since I was very young. I considered majoring in something related to computers after graduating high school, but chose to major in Mathematics, which is my favorite subject. I changed my major years later to Computer Science because I wanted to learn coding and pursue a career in this field.
I am currently in a Software/Web Development course through Correlation One, sponsored by Amazon. I joined this course due to my interest in Computer Science. Software Development is the main field that I'm interested in, so after looking through our company's Career Choice site, that offers PLENTY of opportunities for further education of many fields, I found this program and felt that I would be a great fit due to my background. Also, with everything that the program offers outside of the curriculum such as professional development resources, assistance with job placement, and more, I knew that this opportunity would help propel me further.
We started out with learning HTML, which was fun. We have just finished learned about CSS, which was exciting as it makes styling our pages SO MUCH better and easier! I can't wait to create more expansive works as I hone my skills to show all of you! Thank you as well for visiting my site.
